The Current

Pravah means flow.
This July, it's SKIT's.

Pravah is the annual techno-cultural fest of Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management & Gramothan, Jaipur — three days where music, code, dance and design converge into one current.

From hackathons that run past midnight to pro nights that fill the amphitheatre, Pravah brings together students from colleges across Rajasthan for competitions, performances and the kind of chaos you'll be talking about till next July.
